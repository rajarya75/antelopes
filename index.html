first-of-its-kind Multiverse Living

The multi-dimensional and multi-sensory experiences at Merusri Antelopes redefine modern living through Layers of Wonder Living. Inspired by the captivating beauty of the Antelope Canyon in Arizona, this thoughtfully crafted residential project brings together the depth of human experiences and the brilliance of architectural intelligence. Designed by 2Plus Design, the project transcends traditional living spaces by creating a harmonious blend of nature, wellness, and community.

VILLAS THAT
MAKE YOU
WONDER

Built on the principle of openness, these villas provide expansive plots ranging from 2,200 sq ft to 3,900 sq ft, with G+2 storeys of thoughtfully curated spaces. Natural light flows effortlessly through every corner, while integrating flora and community-driven layouts fosters a sense of connection and harmony. These multifaceted homes invite you to explore, grow, and embrace the beauty of abundant living, making you yearn for what lies beyond the horizon.

Location

ALL OF LIFE,
WITHIN YOUR
REACH

Devanahalli is rapidly transforming into India’s next hub for innovation and growth, with key projects like KHIR City and SEZ Aerospace Park redefining its landscape. These developments foster a thriving ecosystem for research, healthcare, aerospace, and advanced industries, while seamless connectivity through STRR, PRR, and metro expansions ensures easy access to the city’s urban core. Situated in the serene yet strategically connected Brigade Orchards, North Bangalore blends the tranquility of its green surroundings with the vibrancy of cutting-edge infrastructure, making it an unparalleled destination for modern living and investment.
